    Practical storage space solutions for any kitchen

    With our various options for units, pull-outs and even utility rooms, you can get the most out of your kitchen and keep everything perfectly organised. Be inspired – discover intelligent storage space solutions from German Kitchen Studio!

    Discover numerous ideas for optimal kitchen organisation.

    Order with a system

    Check out our clever ideas for cutlery trays and universal organisers as well as the innovative SpaceFlexx system. With these practical tools, your kitchen utensils are always within reach. Our high-quality cutlery trays make child’s play of organising your drawer. Perfectly proportioned, they guarantee plenty of space for even large kitchen utensils like soup ladles and egg whisks. With nobilia’s universal organisers, you can divide up your pull-out entirely according to how you wish to arrange your pans, plates and supplies. Thanks to the elastic dividers in the clever SpaceFlexx system, every container is securely stowed. For convenience you won’t want to be without.

    Clever tall unit solutions

    With their well-thought-out interior organisation and advanced mechanisms, the highly configurable tall units from German Kitchen Studio ensure you get considerably more storage space in your kitchen. Our tall unit systems are therefore especially interesting for anyone without a separate utility room or a basement in their home. Our smart organisational systems give you plenty of space for stowing supplies and household appliances directly within reach in your kitchen. Whether you need somewhere to store bulky drink crates or a broom and ironing board – nobilia offers versatile options for all different scenarios.

    Storage solutions that get you out of a tight corner

    With our holistically planned corner units, you no longer need to “mind the gap” but can instead fully utilise every centimetre of your kitchen based on your needs. For the perfect use of corner space, we offer you innovative solutions with corner units such as the LeMans from Kesseböhmer. Our recommendation: increase the usable storage space by up to 70% with the height-adjustable swivel pull-out.
