    About German Kitchan Studio

    With over 15 years of experience, German Kitchen Studio, Inc. is your premier Nobilia distributor in California and nationwide. We specialize in bringing modern European designs to the USA, transforming kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces with top-tier quality and contemporary styles. Experience the perfect blend of functionality and elegance as we create spaces that inspire and elevate your lifestyle.

    From the living room to the dining room to the kitchen and bathroom: our flexible furniture system and the large selection of fronts and surfaces make it possible to create your home in a consistent style or in a completely different style for each room.
