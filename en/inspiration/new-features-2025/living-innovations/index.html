    Genuine wood veneer: naturally unique

    Our genuine wood veneer furniture in brushed Native Oak features a delicate grain and vivid wood textures that will give each piece of furniture a truly unique look. Each surface is a unique piece and practically impossible to distinguish from solid wood just by looking at it. With a warm presence and pleasant feel, genuine wood veneer furniture makes any space more inviting and a true pleasure to live in. The genuine wood veneer furniture also looks naturally harmonious and impresses with its long-lasting quality. 

    Stand-alone furniture as an eye-catcher

    Whether as a wall panel, sideboard, cabinet or highboard - genuine wood veneer furniture blends seamlessly into your interior and gives every room a warm and inviting touch.
    Stylish accents can be created in combination with solid-coloured cabinet elements from our current collection.

    High-quality veneered coffee and dining tables

    Our genuine wood veneer table tops with their natural wood texture and their stylish Swiss edge are an eye-catcher with a strong personality.
