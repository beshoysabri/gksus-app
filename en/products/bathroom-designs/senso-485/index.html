Urban lightness

The interplay between the delicate colour Sky and light-coloured wood surfaces, such as Oak Como reproduction, gives the bathroom an urban lightness and simple elegance. Soft transitions between the elements make the room appear open and inviting. Black accents, for example in the form of handles or countertop washbasins, create modern contrasts and give the design an urban sophistication.

A special highlight: console plates for countertop washbasins become eye-catchers and underline the stylish combination of Sky and matt black details. This design combines nature and modernity to create a unique place of relaxation and well-being.
