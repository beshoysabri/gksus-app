    Sliding door systems

    Sliding doors are variable all-rounders when it comes to effectively utilising space in limited areas. They enable new room concepts and create a spacious look. As a design element, this exclusive form of sliding and sliding doors can be used perfectly as room dividers as well as for built-in wardrobes and walk-in wardrobes.

    Advantages of sliding doors

    Flexibility

    Sliding and sliding doors can be manufactured in various sizes and therefore offer a wide range of possible applications in the kitchen, bathroom or living room. They can also be used as room dividers to separate or open up a room as required.

    Aesthetics

    Sliding doors can give a modern and elegant appearance. They can be seamlessly integrated into the design of a room and provide a smooth, continuous surface without being interrupted by hinges or handles.

    Space saving

    As sliding doors move sideways and do not pivot inwards or outwards, they do not require any additional space for the pivoting area. This makes them ideal for areas with limited space, such as narrow corridors or small rooms.

    Room flow

    By seamlessly integrating sliding doors into the architecture of a room, they can improve the flow of space by creating an open atmosphere or ensuring privacy in different areas of a home or office.
