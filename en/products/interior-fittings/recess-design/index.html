    Design ideas for recesses.

    Decorative and functional wall views.

    Elegant panel walls with functional elements, decorative wall shelves, clever ideas for recesses – it is this easy to design attractive recesses.

    Niche cladding with motifs.

    Niche claddings with motifs are a trendy and practical way to highlight recesses. nobilia offers you various motifs—for every individual taste and kitchen style.

    Niche cladding with décors.

    Niche cladding is available in the following designs: carcase colours, COLOR CONCEPT colours, worktop décors, high gloss décors and in stainless steel appearance.

    Glass niche cladding.

    Niche cladding made of glass is very exclusive and an extravagant eye-catcher. It is ideal as a splashback for the cooker or at the sink and is virtually effortless to clean thanks to the easy-care surface.

    Shelf unit back panels.

    The design possibilities with shelf unit back panels are extremely versatile and variable. Whether with single colours, with colour accents from COLOR CONCEPT or with graphic motifs—the combination possibilities are virtually unlimited.

    Railing systems.

    The shapely railing systems provide ideal storage for all those kitchen utensils that should be readily accessible.
