    Perfect storage space for every kitchen

    Storage space made for me

    A well-organised kitchen ensures greater efficiency, convenience and aesthetics. With the innovative storage space solutions from nobilia, you can create more space and organisation without compromising on design or functionality. Whether you have a compact kitchen or a spacious eat-in kitchen - our versatile range for interior organisation adapts to your needs and makes optimum use of every centimetre.

    Systematic organisation:

    Drawer and pull-out system

    The basis of every well-organised kitchen is a well thought-out drawer and pull-out system. With nBOX, we are setting new standards in terms of storage space, convenience and space utilisation.

    nBOX – premium design & comfort:

    • Elegant, minimalist design and narrow drawer frame of just 10.7 mm
    • Optionally with closed sides, glass sides or the classic railing as standard
    • Stylish design profiles and design covers for customising the nBOX drawers and pull-outs
    • Push-to-open system for effortless opening and gentle closing in handleless kitchens

    Passage door
