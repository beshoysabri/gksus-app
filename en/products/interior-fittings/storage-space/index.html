    Storage space wonder kitchen

    You will love all the extra space you have in a nobilia kitchen. Have a look for yourself.

    Individual features

    Organising storage space demands space and structure. Our planning grid enables problem-free, individual features, a harmonious storage concept and a variety of personal design options for all kitchen models.

    Pull-out organisation

    Various organisation systems for drawers and pull-outs provide structured storage space for anything you may want to store in your kitchen. From cutlery to cling film and spices, baking trays and even complete crates of bottles - anything fits. The systems are available in variable designs - easy-care synthetic materials, elegant solid wood or a combination of both.

    Pantry Box

    The organiser Pantry Box by Hailo is the ideal storage space for vegetables, bread and the like. A portable plastic box with an inserted mesh base for ventilation ensures that your groceries are stored in ideal conditions. Suitable for 60 cm wide pull-outs.

    Tall unit for drink crates

    Finally space for bulky drink crates. This design makes it possible to store crates on the backward sloping holders, making the bottles easy to see and remove. The tall unit is 600 mm wide and offers three holders for different cabinets. Each fixture can bear loads of up to 35 kg, so depending on the crate size, it is possible to store up to two crates per holder. The holder is also available individually as an accessory.

    Larder unit Junior

    Your kitchen has no space for a standard larder unit? Simply pick its little brother! The base unit with the larder pull-out Junior features 2 adjustable hanging baskets with wooden bottoms and a metal railing.

    Drawer organisation

    Our wide range of plastic and wooden cutlery trays provides you with the finest in drawer organisation. They are suitable for any drawer width from 300 mm to 1,200 mm with a side depth of 561 mm. Several cutlery trays are also available for a side depth of 461 mm.

    Larder unit

    The larder unit with cushioned opening and closing glides quietly in and out of the carcase until the movement is gently damped. The hanging baskets are available in two different designs.

    Full pull-out with 2 metal baskets

    In a narrow space of only 150 mm, for example adjacent to the cooker or hot plate, everything is easily accessible in this unit. The ideal place for storing oil and vinegar or spices.

    Electric all-purpose slicer

    Our range of accessories includes an electric all-purpose slicer for drawer widths of 500 mm and more. The all-purpose slicer can be folded up and stowed in a drawer to save space.

    Internal drawers

    Additional internal drawers can be integrated in front pull-out and revolving door type units. The internal drawer behind the front pull-outs is simultaneously also opened via a follower mechanism.

    Corner units

    Space in even the smallest corner. With nobilia corner units, you have absolute planning reliability due to alternative dimensions, customisable convenience and amazing storage access.

    SpaceFlexx®

    The flexible organisation system for storage containers

    Every kitchen has that one drawer filled with chaos: storage containers of all sizes and shapes, stacked, sorted and abandoned. Now say goodbye to chaos! SpaceFlexx®, our flexible organisation system, will keep your drawer tidy. Flexible dividers keep order among the usual pile of storage containers. Virtually any kitchen utensil can be stored in SpaceFlexx®. Storage containers can no longer tip over or fall out and anything you might need becomes easy to find and reach. SpaceFlexx® can be built into any drawer of at least 60 cm frontal width and 47.2 cm drawer depth. The flexible divider nesttex® Inside is elastic and adjusts to fit the contours and sizes of the stored goods. This way, SpaceFlexx® can flexibly hold utensils of different sizes and shapes securely in their place. nesttex® Inside is dishwasher-safe for convenient cleaning.
