    Modern living space designs

    CREATIVELY COMBINED WITH ANTI-FINGERPRINT FRONTS IN MINERAL GREEN AND TAUPE GREY

    Enjoy greener living in the city! Mineral green elements with grey accents give this living space design its modern natural appeal. The kitchen is truly an all-rounder in urban life, thanks to versatile storage space solutions and creative details like pole-mounted shelving with plant grow lights for sustainability in everyday life. The small pantry is subtly concealed behind access doors. The functional wardrobe solution by the entrance integrates perfectly into the open-plan space. A special highlight feature is created by the combination of both Easytouch fronts, which impress with their velvety matt look and easy-care anti-fingerprint coating.

    Curve for kitchens, bathrooms and living rooms

    The new Curve design gives SENSO and EASYTOUCH an even more unique touch in kitchens, bathrooms and living rooms: The curved look of the front creates the perfect transition to the joint dimension of tall units with a vertical LINE N profile. The end units with their curved contours are a great choice for a surprising number of designs and absolutely ideal for end-of-run and island designs. They are available in widths of 450, 600 and 900 mm. Height and width adjustments are not possible.
