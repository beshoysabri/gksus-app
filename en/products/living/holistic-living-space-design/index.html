    Holistic living space design

    Lots of inspiration for holistic living space and design concepts with unique personality

    Perfectly coordinated kitchen, bathroom and living area

    Our home is more than just our own four walls. Only there do we get that feeling on arrival – that special sense of security and belonging. With our variable and easily combinable interior elements, you can now organise your home to suit your very personal lifestyle. Individual, flexible, contemporary and holistic. A versatile furniture system designed to bring lots of your interior ideas to life – and open up new perspectives. One home, one style! 

    Modern living space designs

    CREATIVELY COMBINED WITH ANTI-FINGERPRINT FRONTS IN MINERAL GREEN AND TAUPE GREY

    Enjoy greener living in the city! Mineral green elements with grey accents give this living space design its modern natural appeal. The kitchen is truly an all-rounder in urban life, thanks to versatile storage space solutions and creative details like pole-mounted shelving with plant grow lights for sustainability in everyday life. The small pantry is subtly concealed behind access doors. The functional wardrobe solution by the entrance integrates perfectly into the open-plan space. A special highlight feature is created by the combination of both Easytouch fronts, which impress with their velvety matt look and easy-care anti-fingerprint coating.

    MORE INFO ON ANTI-FINGERPRINT FRONTS

    From the living room and dining room to the kitchen and bathroom: thanks to the large selection of fronts, surfaces, chairs and dining tables, you can get everything for your home from a single source.

    Kitchen and dining area: a spacious comfort zone for friends and family

    Simply enjoy with a flavourful base.

    A subtle mix of silk grey and grey slate characterises the room. The dining room table reflects the colour concept of the kitchen and is complemented by swivel upholstered chairs. The elegant and cleverly concealed storage space even impresses professionals like Sally.

    Dining tables and chairs

    Discover our versatile dining tables and chairs that are perfect for open-plan kitchens and individual dining rooms. Our dining tables are available in various fixed sizes and optionally extendable, ideal for socialising and special occasions. Complete your planning with our chairs, available in three frame variants and four trendy colours. Create an elegant and practical atmosphere in your home with our furniture.

    Inviting Cottage style harmony

    KITCHEN, HOME OFFICE, LIVING ROOM AND BATHROOM AS AN INTEGRATED WHOLE

    This is how favourite spots look. The new Cottage style gets its refreshing modern radiance from a Nordic blue. True comfort with a touch of that holiday feeling for your living room and bathroom. Tasteful and smart use of kitchen space, with an open-plan, practical workspace directly adjacent. Our Cascada Cottage style front in Fjord blue and White combined with stylish details brings inviting harmony to your home. Perfect for those who love a mix of traditional and Scandinavian!

    Spaciously designed home

    EXCITING MATERIAL MIX OF GLASS APPEARANCE AND OAK

    What starts in the kitchen is continued into the living and dining areas. With nobilia, you get a coherent look thanks to décors and ranges that work in all corners of your home. This spacious living and dining area is open and inviting. A particular sense of harmony is achieved over the entire space through recurring wood accents in Sierra oak reproduction and clean white in an elegant glass appearance, complemented by cool elements in black. For comfortable living in a contemporary design!

    Our Artis front in a high-quality glass appearance in Alpine white is guaranteed to turn heads in this designer kitchen. A sharp contrast is created by the matt, deep black Xtra Ceramic worktop. For an impressively high-quality interior that is as modern as it is timeless.

    The sideboard impresses with matt satin fronts in a striking glass appearance and white handles. The bevelled shelf unit delivers a natural oak look, while the individual Sign Emotion railing system in aluminium provides a very modern design in black.

    Love harmony and comfort? Then create a holistic feel-good experience in your bathroom and dressing room. Open wardrobe units with a well thought-out interior are concealed behind expansive sliding doors.

    An elegant welcome. The floating sideboard in ultra matt black with handles in a matching shade is complemented by the plug-in wall shelf in an oak look. Combine with a large mirror that blends in harmoniously.

    The perfect match. Dark surfaces and well-designed handles in matt black and cool metal give the kitchen its individual character. The ceramic niche cladding and matching worktop in Metal oxide décor are easy to care for and uncomplicated. A strong solution, and not just for fans of metal.

    Looking for more spaciousness and harmony? Simply bring the elements in the Bergamo oak look and Graphite black décors through to the living space.     Easytouch (966) / Havana oak reproduction (097)

    The simple design comprising sideboard and shelving elements not only provides a fresh look and an exceptional and well thought-out flexible storage space concept, but also has impressive attention to detail, such as the compartment for set-top boxes and similar items, which is available in 30 colours.

    Riva (842)

    Pure harmony with perfectly coordinated fronts that create a uniform look. Wonderful details: the Boulevard cabinet with glass doors and concrete fronts in a subtle sand shade.

    Artis (938) / Sierra oak reproduction (198)

    Artis (937) / Structura (405)

    Easytouch (967) / Easytouch (961)

    Light meets dark, creating a clear, urban aesthetic. Basics for the look: units and shelf units with black carcase and easy-care anti-fingerprint fronts. Glass as a design element provides additional lightness.

    Touch (338) / Riva (839)

    This sideboard arrangement was designed to stand on the floor, a very contemporary interpretation of storage space. A sense of harmony is achieved by satin grey elements with deliberately simple handles combined with the warm concrete shade Terra grey. A definite highlight in the living area: the round upright panels.

    Riva (840)

    A living trend that creates a truly exclusive style with exciting material contrasts, a minimalistic design language and dark tones. The combination of modern walnut décor and shiny black make a real statement.

    Easytouch (969) / Riva (840)
