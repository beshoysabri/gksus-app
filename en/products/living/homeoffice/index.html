    MODERN FEEL-GOOD WORKSPACE

    FEEL-GOOD OFFICE

    WORK MORE BEAUTIFULLY WITH OUR HOME OFFICE SOLUTIONS – PERFECTLY INTEGRATED INTO YOUR HOME

    More and more of us are working from home. With nobilia, you can create a modern workspace with the perfect setup for your requirements. For a home office with attractive furnishings – nobilia has what you need! Our modular furniture concepts enable you to link your working and living spaces in style. We want you to simply work more beautifully from home. No matter how big or small the available space, you can perfectly integrate an office setup into any home with the right design.

    Laser (414) / Oak Montreal reproduction (126)

    Working from home in the heart of the city is an exciting way of living that offers a huge amount of flexibility. Indispensable: an intelligently structured kitchen that makes improvising and organising very easy, even in a smaller space. Healthy lunch break at your home office included.

    Laser (414) / Oak Montreal reproduction (126)

    Everything perfectly integrated and stylish. Separate the home office from the kitchen and living area using opaque sliding doors that can also be left open to reveal a real eye-catcher. The unique design ensures harmony throughout.

    Laser (414) / Oak Montreal reproduction (126)

    Everything readily accessible! The generous shelving provides plenty of space for work documents, books and much more.

    Oak Montreal reproduction (126)

    Carefully considered storage options and shelves adapt to your working style. So that you can focus on the task to hand. The bevelled shelf unit with its interesting contour and astoundingly authentic natural look, very close to the genuine wood that inspires it, ensures even more variety here.

    Oak Montreal reproduction (126)

    Stay relaxed while working! Benefit from a modern workplace designed to best suit your particular needs.

    Easytouch (967) / Vikings Oak reproduction 205

    No matter how compact the room, there’s still space for a home office. Thanks to our versatile furniture concepts, you get everything you need here: a functional desk combined with storage units and work surfaces.

    Easytouch (966)

    Create coherence! This home office design in Fjord blue is perfectly integrated into the adjacent kitchen. The ceiling-height wall units make optimum use of the space and offer plenty of storage. Open shelves combined with a cork pinboard in the niches produce an exciting mix and visually soften the design.

    Easytouch (966) / Concrete Sand reproduction (201)

    Creative chaos in the home office? Nothing of the sort! Pens, art supplies, books and all the rest are easily stored thanks to the spaciously designed desk solution comprising a worktop and base units with pull-outs and shelves.

    Riva (839)

    This designed workspace from our Riva range combines conventional storage solutions such as shelves and visual highlights such as glass wall units with an extended worktop. The result: a place to sit down and quickly grab your laptop or whatever else!

    Easytouch (966) / Havana oak reproduction (097)
