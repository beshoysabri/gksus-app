    Modern furniture for your living room

    COSY DETAILS YOU’LL FALL IN LOVE WITH

    Comfortable media furniture, modern glass cabinets, a variety of coffee tables – these and other furnishing elements from our living assortment perfectly complement your living room and offer lots of interior design options. Discover living ideas you’ll fall in love with and furnish your space according to how you live your life. For an all-round feeling of well-being at home!

    Naturally individual

    The natural interplay of the matt white of our popular EASYTOUCH anti-fingerprint front and authentic oak-like decor has an inviting and harmonious effect. It lets variable elements such as the coffee table and matching glass cabinets and sideboards come together to form the perfect ensemble for individualists. 

    The choice is yours! Add a sideboard with a media bridge to your hanging glass cabinet with glass inlays. It features a particularly handy cable opening which lets any cable disappear with ease.

    The black steel plinth foot base is a key sign of modern living trends and helps the furniture appear as if it is light and floating.

    Combined harmoniously

    Oh, happy grey! Our new taupe grey is homely and can be combined in versatile ways. Discover an eye-catching item of your own, such as the free-standing glass cabinet with glass corners on both sides or media console with an indirectly illuminated media panel. Its cable opening makes all cables disappear visually.

    The new coffee tables with an elegant framework-style base come across as highly tasteful. Available in two sizes and with various tabletop decors.

    Glass cabinets: a classic. They can be installed in various ways, offering a vast array of design possibilities. With a choice of clear, matt or black glass.

    FURTHER LIVING SPACE DESIGNS

     

     

    Coffee Table Collection

    There are many gorgeous possibilities for giving a living space more personality. You can give our new coffee tables an individual design. Take your pick of three base models, each available in two sizes, and a wide range of colours and decors.

    DUBLIN COFFEE TABLE

    Minimalism, pure and clean lines – the ideal coffee table for a modern ambience. The base is a black underframe made of powdered steel, including furniture pads. In addition, it can be combined individually with a tabletop available in various decors and a 16, 25 or 50 mm thickness. Available in 120 × 80 cm and 80 × 80 cm dimensions.

    Perfect for any lover of beautiful objects.

    The design language is modern and yet homely at the same time. Available in all matt carcase colours and ultra-matt front decors in a 120 x 80 cm and 80 x 80 cm size.

    DISCOVER FRONT DECORS

     

    DENVER FACETED COFFEE TABLE

    Our coffee tables make a statement thanks to their facets and geometric shapes. Practical: the coffee table can be positioned flexibly thanks to its castors and provides additional storage space.

    DETROIT FACETED COFFEE TABLE

    The purest form of design. An elegant way of enriching any space. The table frame made of black powdered steel has a light and airy effect. Shown here in combination with a velvety-matt anti-fingerprint surface in white and rust red.

    Media furniture with a system

    Modern media technology, especially televisions and sound bars, are usually an important hub in the living room. With our new media furniture, we offer a series of more attractive and practical solutions, cable management included.

    MEDIA PANEL (01.) AND MEDIA CONSOLE (02.)

    Practical in function, beautiful in design! In addition to a TV mounting bracket, the media panel offers a spacer frame for discreetly hiding cables. The optional LED design showcases your TV and provides atmospheric lighting. Hi-fi equipment and remotes can be easily accommodated in the media console on the base unit. The media panel and console are available in all carcase colours, the cable feed-through in black or stainless steel appearance.

    MEDIA BRIDGE WITH CABLE FEED-THROUGH

    Practical TV riser and extra storage option for receiver, sound bar and more. Our media bridge offers more integrated cable management on base units: thanks to cable feed-throughs in the back panel and in the top shelf, cables disappear behind the floating back panel. The media bridge is available in all carcase colours, the cable feed-through in black or stainless steel appearance.
