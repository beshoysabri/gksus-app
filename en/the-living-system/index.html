    The system for more individuality

    Versatile layout options, optimum storage concepts and a holistically harmonious and timeless design language – with our new n144 grid, we’ve developed a system that greatly simplifies kitchen planning and offers many benefits.
    This vibrant and flexible kitchen concept leaves nothing to be desired – give your creativity free rein with the new features in the 2023 collection.

    Perfection in form and function

    The soup ladle makes the difference.

    144 mm – the height of our drawers.  This measurement represents the fundamental unit on which our grid system is based. High enough to store even large kitchen utensils such as soup ladles and egg whisks in a drawer, but still sufficiently slimline for a refined and elegant look. The new benchmark against which everything else is measured – n144.

    Clever storage space.
    Perfectly organised.

    With the new nobilia grid, the sky’s the limit when it comes to getting your kitchen in perfect order. Our wall and tall units can be flexibly configured, while the 2023 collection also offers new organisational possibilities and innovative storage solutions – such as a special fixture for beverage crates. What makes our grid system so special is that the units can be planned right up to the ceiling. Ensuring you get the most out of your space.

    DISCOVER MORE STORAGE SOLUTIONS

    Ideal ergonomics thanks to flexible height system.

    Our flexible height system makes working heights between 66.2 cm and 110.2 cm possible, with four carcase heights to choose from. Built-in appliances can now also be configured in tall units at your desired height without increased planning complexity. Making baking all the more pleasurable!

    Harmonious design.
    Uniform lines.

    In addition to its practical benefits, the n144 grid also boasts impressive aesthetics. The optimally matched carcase heights of the base, wall and tall units create the perfect symmetry and harmonious lines, with large fronts in particular coming even further into their own.  A holistic concept that can be continued in any room and is also particularly effective in design-oriented planning.

    Versatile grid. New design options.

    The exciting new features in our 2023 collection also blend perfectly into the grid. From continuous recessed handles and exclusive electrical appliances through to extra-tall XL units – everything can be effortlessly combined. Design highlights like our new fronts and décors top everything off and offer maximum flexibility for your design preferences.
